About this deck

This page embeds the official Twitch and YouTube players directly — there are no ads, trackers, or analytics of its own here.

Ads that appear inside a Twitch or YouTube player come from Twitch/YouTube's own servers, same as on twitch.tv or youtube.com. The "Ad Shield" toggle keeps new/reloaded tiles muted by default so ad audio doesn't surprise you, but no page script can reach inside an official cross-origin player to strip ads out of it.

Twitch/YouTube-specific blockers (Pie Adblock, Twitch Adblock, etc.) work by rewriting the video manifest or intercepting ad requests on the real twitch.tv/youtube.com domain — most are built to only activate when that's the page's actual top-level address, so they typically won't reach into an embed on a third-party domain like this one, no matter how the embed itself is built. Every tile now has an open-on-native-site button (the ↗ icon) that opens that exact stream on the real twitch.tv/youtube.com page in a new tab, where your ad blocker's Twitch/YouTube filters are actually designed to run.

EMBED PARENT DOMAIN

If a Twitch tile shows a grey "parent" error, this page is running somewhere Twitch doesn't recognize as a real host (e.g. opened directly as a file://). Serve it from any domain, or a local dev server like python3 -m http.server, and it'll resolve.
